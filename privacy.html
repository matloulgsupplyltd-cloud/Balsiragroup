How BALSIRA GROUP collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and business information submitted through our website and portal, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, company details, and supplier registration information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used to manage procurement enquiries, communicate with suppliers and clients, process portal access requests, and comply with applicable legal requirements.

3. Data Protection

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse in accordance with applicable South African (POPIA) and UK data protection laws.

We may share limited data with trusted third parties strictly for service delivery purposes. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Requests can be sent to info@balsira.com

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of any changes. Last updated: 2026.
