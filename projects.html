Mining Equipment Procurement — Zambia Copper Belt

Balsira facilitated procurement of heavy earthmoving and processing equipment for a copper mining operation in Zambia's Copperbelt Province. The scope included sourcing excavators, conveyors, and crushing equipment from verified suppliers.

Cross-border logistics coordination from South Africa into Zambia was managed end-to-end, with compliance documentation handled by the Balsira team.

LocationCopperbelt Province, Zambia
ScopeEquipment sourcing, supplier qualification, logistics coordination

Railway Track Components Supply — Tanzania

Balsira supported procurement of railway track components for an infrastructure upgrade project in Tanzania. This included sourcing rail sections, concrete sleepers, fastening systems, and fishplates from qualified international suppliers.

The project leveraged Balsira's East Africa market access and supplier network to meet TBS compliance requirements and project timelines.

Industrial ProcurementSouth Africa

Industrial Pumps & Valves — Mining Operation, Limpopo

Multi-phase procurement of industrial pumping systems and process valves for a platinum mining operation in Limpopo, South Africa. Balsira identified and qualified three competing suppliers before recommending and placing the order.

The agency model allowed the client to benefit from Balsira's supplier relationships and volume negotiations without managing the procurement process internally.
