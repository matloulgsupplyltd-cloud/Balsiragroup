About Balsira

Industrial Procurement
Across Africa

BALSIRA GROUP is an industrial procurement company focused on sourcing mining equipment, railway engineering solutions, and metals supply for high-demand sectors across Africa — operating as a supplier-to-customer bridge through a distributor and agency model.

Purpose
Mission & Vision

Connecting Africa's industrial sectors with verified equipment suppliers through structured procurement.

Our Mission

To be Africa's leading industrial procurement facilitator in mining and railway engineering — providing efficient, ethical, and compliant equipment sourcing while connecting verified suppliers with institutional buyers across Southern and East Africa.

We strive to reduce procurement risk, streamline supply chains, and deliver measurable operational efficiency for all stakeholders.

Our Vision

To become the primary procurement and distribution partner for mining and railway infrastructure projects across the African continent — enabling sustainable economic development by linking industrial demand and supply efficiently and transparently.

Our goal is to build a trusted network of suppliers, distributors, and institutional clients that accelerates African industrial growth.

How We Work
Our Role In The Value Chain

Balsira operates as a middleman and agency — bridging suppliers and manufacturers to institutional buyers through structured procurement delivery.

01

Identify Needs

We analyse client projects and market requirements, providing insights to optimise sourcing strategies and reduce procurement inefficiencies.

02

Source Products

We identify qualified suppliers and manufacturers for mining and railway equipment, validating quality, compliance, and logistics capability.

03

Evaluate Suppliers

Independent assessment of supplier submissions ensures ethical and efficient decision-making while maintaining market neutrality.

04

Coordinate Delivery

End-to-end procurement coordination including logistics, cross-border compliance, and supplier performance monitoring.
